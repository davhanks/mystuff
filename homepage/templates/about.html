We started as photographers in traditional sense. We still are; we teach classes, we serve in our disciplines, we take photos, and we record films. We understand what it means to be photographers.


Since becoming photographers, we had watched cameras prices steadily increase even while manufactoring and distribution costs decreased.


We also realized the market for cameras was shifting. Traditional cameras were being enveloped by cheaper options that did not offer the flexibility of a professional camera. The art of photography was losing out easy to use equipment.


We decided to start new type of business model in 2013. We strive for equipment that not only can be used by the world's best photographers, but also that can remain low cost.
